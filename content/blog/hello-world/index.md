I'm incredibly excited as I write this blog and mark the start of my blogging journey.

In the past few months, people around the world have suffered tremendous losses.

As I write this blogpost, COVID19 is raging across the globe, numerous places in India are experiencing extreme weather conditions, Beirut has witnessed a 2700+ ton Ammonium Nitrate explosion and the Apple Fire has forced thousands to flee in California. Safe to say, 2020 has not been kind to anyone.

However, the past few months of staying at home and college campus shutdown have given me lots of opportunities to learn and expirement with different tools and technologies. I have been able to widen my skills and tinker with lots of new ideas and concepts and I am very grateful and thankful for all these opportunities I have got.

Over the past few months of diving into web development, app development, designing user experiences and even some data analysis and machine learning, I have realised the importance of documenting one's journey. Having a blog will allow me to keep a track of my progress, share my thoughts and ideas and learn to communicate them better.

I am excited to share a piece of my mind with the internet and look forward to writing regularly!
